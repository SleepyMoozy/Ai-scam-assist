AI Scam Assist

An in-progress project to protect a generation.

Project Summary

AI Scam Assist is a safety net for elderly users, designed to protect them from online scams. Our service uses a browser extension to flag suspicious ads with an AI, and then allows users to request a human-verified second opinion from a network of vetted youth. This dual-layered approach combines technology with a personal touch to build a safer online environment.

How It Works

Elderly User

This is the primary user who benefits from the service. They install a browser extension that acts as a first line of defense against online scams, providing peace of mind as they browse the web.

Youth User

A network of vetted, verified youth who provide a second opinion on potentially fraudulent ads, using their digital literacy to ensure accuracy and provide a human touch.

The Algorithm

A machine learning model that analyzes ads for common scam indicators, such as suspicious URLs, unusual text, or misleading images, providing an initial risk score.

Data Reinvestment

Every human verdict from a youth user is fed back into our algorithm, creating a continuous feedback loop that makes the AI smarter and more accurate over time.
